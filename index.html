About Me

A brief introduction to my journey.

My journey in Applied Mathematics at Noakhali Science and Technology University (NSTU) has shaped my analytical mindset and passion for solving complex problems. The rigorous curriculum has given me a strong quantitative foundation and a deep appreciation for the power of mathematics in understanding the world.

As I delved deeper into my studies, I discovered a fascination for programming, data analysis, and research. This spark led me to explore the intersection of mathematics, statistics, and technology—fueling my drive to extract insights from data and build impactful solutions using tools like Python, machine learning, and web development.

Serving as President of the NSTU Research Society, I have led initiatives to foster a vibrant research culture, organized workshops and competitions, and collaborated with faculty and students across disciplines. This leadership experience has taught me the value of teamwork, vision, and empowering others to pursue innovation and discovery.
